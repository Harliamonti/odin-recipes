French Toast

French toast is a delectable dish where bread is soaked in egg yolk and fried until golden brown

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Pepper & Salt

Steps

  1. Put 2 eggs into into a bowl with some pepper & salt and beat
  2. Add a dash of milk and continue to beat until mixed
  3. Cut slice of bread in half and dip in egg batter
  4. Fry battered egg until golden brown
  5. Enjoy!
